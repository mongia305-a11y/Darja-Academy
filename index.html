Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know Arabic?

No. Our courses are designed for complete beginners as well as learners with some Arabic background.

Is this Tunisian Darja or Standard Arabic?

Darja Academy focuses on Tunisian Arabic (Darja), the language spoken in everyday life in Tunisia.

Can I take private lessons?

Yes! Private one-on-one lessons are available through the Private Lessons section.
